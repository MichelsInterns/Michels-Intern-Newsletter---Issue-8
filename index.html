| Pinata Delivery
Milwaukee, WI
Delivering smiles one pinata at a time
Last week, interns from Brownville and Lomira helped deliver Piñatas to the Children's Hospital in Milwaukee, WI — bringing color, creativity, and a whole lot of smiles.

After snapping a few photos at the hospital, some of the interns swung by our office for a behind-the-scenes tour and lunch on the rooftop patio (yes, the views lived up to the hype).

From giving back to the community to getting a taste of our workspace culture in Milwaukee, it was a day full of connection, inspiration, and fresh air.

ON THE RISE
MIchels canada
Michels Canada provides Canada with clean energy solutions while protecting its vast natural resources. The company has built the strongest team and established the best safety culture to deliver reliable heavy civil, trenchless, and pipeline solutions. They stake a claim as the country’s best in pursuing a safer, cleaner, and more sustainable future for Canada.

Project profile
COQUITLAM, BC
Michels Canada is installing 1.45 km of 3.2 m (10.5-foot) diameter steel water pipeline along Pipeline Road, from Robson Drive to Guildford Way in Coquitlam, BC. Using open-cut construction in a busy urban environment, this project helps meet the region’s growing drinking water demand.
